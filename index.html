Frequently Asked Questions

What are the advantages of HDPE pipes over traditional materials?

HDPE pipes offer superior corrosion resistance, flexibility, lightweight construction, and longer service life compared to PVC, steel, or concrete pipes.

What pressure ratings are available?

Our HDPE pipes are available in various pressure ratings, typically ranging from PN 6 to PN 25, depending on the SDR (Standard Dimension Ratio) required for your project.

How are HDPE pipes joined?

They are primarily joined using heat fusion techniques such as butt fusion or electrofusion, creating a leak-proof, monolithic joint that is stronger than the pipe itself.

Are HDPE pipes suitable for potable water?

Yes, our pipes are manufactured from food-grade PE100 material, making them completely safe, non-toxic, and certified for transporting potable drinking water.

What is the expected service life?

When installed according to industry standards, HDPE pipes have an expected service life of 50 to 100 years due to their high resistance to corrosion and environmental stress.
